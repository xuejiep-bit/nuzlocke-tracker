Black Nuzlocke Encounter Guide

Knowing which routes have the best encounters is crucial. Here are the must-know catches in Pokemon Black.

Early Game (Badges 1-3)

Route 1 - Lillipup (S-tier): Stoutland is one of the best Pokemon in the entire game. Intimidate ability, great bulk, strong STAB Return. Do not overlook this common encounter.

Route 3 - Blitzle: Zebstrika is fast and hits hard. Motor Drive gives Electric immunity. Solid team member for the whole game.

Wellspring Cave - Roggenrola: Boldore is an excellent physical tank. Sturdy ability is a built-in Focus Sash that can save you from unexpected OHKOs.

Pinwheel Forest - Timburr: Gurdurr is excellent even without trade evolution. Great Attack stat and bulk for the mid-game.

Mid Game (Badges 4-6)

Desert Resort - Sandile (S-tier): Krookodile with Moxie is a Nuzlocke monster. Ground/Dark typing gives amazing coverage. One of the best Pokemon in any Unova Nuzlocke.

Desert Resort - Darumaka (S-tier): Darmanitan has the highest Attack stat of any non-legendary in Unova. Sheer Force makes it a nuclear option against anything that does not resist Fire.

Chargestone Cave - Joltik: Galvantula with Compound Eyes plus Thunder has 91% accuracy. Fast special attacker with unique Bug/Electric coverage.

Route 6 - Deerling: Sawsbuck is surprisingly good with Sap Sipper (Grass immunity), Horn Leech for recovery, and Jump Kick for coverage.

Late Game (Badges 7-8 + E4)

Twist Mountain - Cubchoo: Beartic provides crucial Ice-type coverage for Drayden Dragon gym. Not amazing stats but fills a critical role.

Route 9 - Pawniard: Bisharp is incredibly strong. Dark/Steel typing with Defiant ability can sweep entire teams.

Victory Road - Mienfoo: Mienshao is fast and powerful. Regenerator keeps it healthy throughout long Elite Four runs.
