Why Use a Dedicated FireRed Nuzlocke Tracker?

Pokémon FireRed is the most recommended game for a first Nuzlocke. Kanto's 35+ routes and dungeons require careful encounter tracking. This tracker comes with all FireRed routes pre-loaded — from Route 1 to Victory Road and everything in between.

FireRed Nuzlocke Quick Tips

Best Starter: Squirtle for safety, Bulbasaur for Sleep Powder utility. See our FireRed Tier List for complete rankings.

Key Encounters: Nidoran♂ (Route 3 — evolve early with Moon Stone), Abra (Route 5), Magikarp (any water), Snorlax (Route 12/16). These are game-changing encounters.

Hardest Gym: Sabrina's Alakazam is extremely fast and powerful. Bring Shadow Ball or a bulky Dark/Normal type. Check our FireRed Gym Leaders guide for strategies.

Champion: Blue's team is diverse and depends on your starter. You need balanced type coverage to handle everything he throws at you.
