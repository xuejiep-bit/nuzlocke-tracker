Use Ground types — they are completely immune to Electric. Marshtomp or Geodude make this gym free. Danger: Voltorb knows Selfdestruct and WILL use it. Do NOT leave a low-HP Pokémon in against it. Magneton is Electric/Steel so Ground is 4× effective.

#4

Flannery

Lavaridge Town
FireLevel Cap: 29
PokémonLevelType
NumelLv. 24Fire Ground
SlugmaLv. 24Fire
CameruptLv. 26Fire Ground
TorkoalLv. 29Fire
Nuzlocke Strategy

Use Water or Ground types. Marshtomp is the MVP here — Water + Ground hits everything super effectively. Danger: Torkoal's Overheat hits incredibly hard on the first use, but lowers its Sp.Atk afterward. Survive the first Overheat and you win. Numel and Camerupt are 4× weak to Water.

#5

Norman

Petalburg City
NormalLevel Cap: 31
PokémonLevelType
SpindaLv. 27Normal
VigorothLv. 27Normal
LinooneLv. 29Normal
SlakingLv. 31Normal
Nuzlocke Strategy

Use Fighting types. Hariyama or Machoke are ideal. THE HARDEST GYM IN EMERALD. Slaking has enormous Attack and knows Facade. Do NOT burn or paralyze it — Facade doubles in power with status conditions. Exploit Slaking's Truant ability: it loafs every other turn. Use Protect on its attack turns, then hit hard on its Truant turns.

Winona

FlyingLevel Cap: 33

TropiusLv. 29Grass Flying
PelipperLv. 30Water Flying
SkarmoryLv. 31Steel Flying
AltariaLv. 33Dragon Flying
Nuzlocke Strategy

Use Electric, Ice, or Rock types. This is a diverse team — no single type beats everything. Danger: Altaria knows Dragon Dance. If it sets up even once, it can sweep your entire team. Prioritize taking Altaria down fast with Ice moves. Skarmory is very bulky physically — use Special attacks.

PokémonLevelType
ClaydolLv. 41Ground Psychic
XatuLv. 41Psychic Flying
SolrockLv. 42Rock Psychic

Nuzlocke Strategy

This is a Double Battle. Use Dark, Water, or Ghost types. Sharpedo with Crunch is excellent. Danger: Claydol has Earthquake which hits both your Pokémon AND its ally — position carefully. Solrock and Lunatone can use Cosmic Power to become very tanky. Take them down fast before they set up.

Nuzlocke Strategy

Use Grass or Electric types. Major Danger: Kingdra has Water/Dragon typing, meaning its only weakness is Dragon. It also knows Rest to heal. This is one of the toughest fights in Emerald. Whiscash is 4× weak to Grass. Bring a Pokémon with Toxic for Kingdra if you lack Dragon moves.
