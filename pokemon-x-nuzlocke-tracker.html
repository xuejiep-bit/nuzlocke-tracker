X Nuzlocke Quick Tips

Best Starter: Froakie. Greninja's Speed makes it arguably the best Nuzlocke starter ever. You also get a free Kanto starter in Lumiose City.

Key Encounters: Ralts (Route 4), Honedge (Route 6 — Aegislash is broken), Fletchling (Route 2 — Talonflame with Gale Wings). Don't forget your free Lucario!

Mega Evolution: Plan which Pokémon gets your Mega Stone. Mega Kangaskhan with Parental Bond is devastatingly powerful.

Champion: Diantha's Mega Gardevoir is the main threat. Steel or Poison types shut her down.
